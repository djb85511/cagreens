Greens in spring 2017 elections

At least six Greens ran in spring 2017 elections, with one elected. Click on the link below underlying each candidates' name to find their campaign home page and the other links for their social media and candidate statements. Note - inclusion on this list does not imply endorsement. Endorsements are made by the county Green Party in question and are listed below where they have occured. Candidates are listed only after they qualify for the ballot.
