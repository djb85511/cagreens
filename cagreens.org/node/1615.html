Endorsed 2022 Statewide Candidates

In a historic decision, the Green Party of California has joined with the Peace and Freedom Party to endorse a joint Left Unity Slate of seven statewide candidates for the upcoming June 2022 election. In addition to advancing our shared values, this cooperative strategy increases the likelihood that both parties will reach 2% of the vote in at least one statewide race, which is a minimum threshold needed to retain ballot status.

But first, our candidates need YOUR help in Gathering Signatures. for their Petitions in Lieu of Filing Fee to get on the ballot. The deadline for submitting signatures is February 9! Learn how you can help here.

Please also reach out and support our Left Unity Slate of candidates listed below and DONATE to our “Statewide Green Candidates Fund”.


 

Luis J Rodriguez

Luis J. Rodriguez.is running as a Green for governor of California. His "Imagine a New California... Then Let's Build It!” campaign goals include addressing homeless, environmental justice, and universal healthcare. His vision is built on his decades of community involvement, from mentoring in the incarcerated community to being the 2014 Poet Laureate in LA. (https://www.luisjrodriguez.com)


  

Gary Blenner

Gary Blenner is running as a Green for CA Secretary of State. He supports rank choice voting, expansion of voting rights, and upholding fairness in our election system. (https://www.facebook.com/100075853868655)


  

Laura Wells

Laura Wells is running as a Green for California Controller in 2022, whose role is to "Follow The Money" and report to the boss (the public). She supports money-saving policies like green jobs, public banking, healthcare-for-all, and much more.  Laura Wells experience include working in the finance field, and decades of social and political activism in building the Green Party of California. (https://laurawells.org/) 

 


Meghann Adams

Meghann Adams of the Peace and Freedom Party is running for California Treasurer and is endorsed by the California Green Party.


 

Dan Kapelovitz

Dan Kapelovitz is running as a Green for California Attorney General in 2022. Kapelovitiz describes his priority as an attorney is helping poor people, people who have been wronged by the system, and defending animal rights. (https://kapelovitz.com)



Nathalie Hrizi

Nathalie Hrizi of the Peace and Freedom Party is running for insurance commissioner and is endorsed by the California Green Party. Hrizi works as a public school teacher and a community activist in San Francisco. She has run for Insurance Commissioner multiple times, most recently earning a respectable 5% of the vote. 



John Parker

John Thompson Parker of the Peace and Freedom Party is endorsed by the Green Party of California in his run for US Senate.
