GPUS Delegation Members, 2018-2020


There are seventeen delegates and seventeen alternates in GPUS delegation, as adjusted by GPUS each effective Monday 9/23/2019. In the last election delegates and alternates were elected for staggered two-year terms for July 1, 2019-June 30, 2021, and July 1, 2018-June 30, 2020.
The GPCA has created the observer position temporarily to include previously elected Alternates until their term ends.
