Delegates

General Assembly delegates are chosen by active County Organizations. Each has at least one delegate, for a total of 58 if all of California's counties are active. Each active County Organization has an additional number of seats out of an additional 100 seats, equal to its percentage of registered Greens from within the county, compared to the total number of registered Greens in all counties, with a minimum of 1% required for one seat. (Reference GPCA Bylaws 7-1)
