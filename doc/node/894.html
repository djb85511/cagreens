Greens in spring 2014 elections

Thirty Greens ran in spring 2014 elections*, with eleven elected. Here is the Green voter guide, containing Green endorsements for statewide propositions and candidates:

Statewide Office (5)

Luis Rodriguez, Governor  (endorsed by the Green Party of California) 
6th/15 for two seats - 66,872 votes, 1.5%

Jena Goodman, Lt. Governor  (endorsed by the Green Party of California) 
6th/8 for two seats - 98,338 votes, 2.4%

David Curtis, Secretary of State  (endorsed by the Green Party of California) 
8th/8 for two seats - 121,618 votes, 3.0%

Laura Wells, Controller  (endorsed by the Green Party of California)  
6th/6 for two seats - 231,352 votes, 5.7%

Ellen Brown, Treasurer  (endorsed by the Green Party of California) 
3rd/3 for two seats - 270,388 votes, 6.6%
