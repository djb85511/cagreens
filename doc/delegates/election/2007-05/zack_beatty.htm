I am interested in running for the position of Delegate to the National Green Party Convention, from the GP of California. I am currently a member of the County Council for the Green Party of Los Angeles County. I am also a member of the State Coordinating Committee of the GPCA. My politics and worldview coincide with the 10 Key Values of the GPCA.

However, I specifically am running for this seat to raise the following political viewpoints to a higher stature, within the Green Party.

* U.S. OUT OF IRAQ!
Complete withdrawal of US forces, including dismantling of military
bases. Expose the fraudulent "War of Terror".

* LOCALIZE NOW!
Relocalize our community economies, food and energy production,
transportation, money, gov't, and media. Post-carbon prep.

* PROTECT CIVIL LIBERTIES!
Repeal the Patriot Act, and stop NSA wiretapping and Internet
monitoring. Restore the Bill of Rights.
