Greens in Fall 2020 Elections

Congratulations to at least nine California Greens who have won their race in the November 2020 Election.

Five CA Green candidates have won contested elections: to Emmanuel Estrada, ​Sylvia R. Chavez, David Grover, Micheal Paul Hansen, ​and Abe Powell.
Four additional Green candidates have won uncontested elections; ​Jane Jarlsberg, ​Randy Marx, ​Matthew Clark, and ​Sharon Parra.

At least 26 California Greens ran for office in 2020. * This listing does not indicate endorsement by the Green Party of California and is for informational purposes.

Howie Hawkins and Angela Walker - Green Party of United States Presidential and Vice Presidential Candidates
https://howiehawkins.us/
Nominated at the Presidential Nominating Convention of July 11th, 2020, receiving 210 of 355 (59.1%) of national delegate votes.
In California's March Primary Election Howie Hawkins received 4,203 votes (36.2%).
In the November General Election Howie Hawkins received 77,599 (0.5%) of California votes, and 381,462 (0.2%) of the national votes.
