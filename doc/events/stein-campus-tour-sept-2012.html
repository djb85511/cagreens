Green Party Presidential Candidate Jill Stein California campus tour September 24-27, 2012

Monday, September 24 (Northern California):

College Of The Redwoods (3:30pm)
7351 Tompkins Hill Rd., Eureka

Humboldt State University (8pm): Party Green with Jill Stein
Kate Buchanan Room, University Center Building - 1 Harpst Street, Arcata
Co-sponsored by the Humboldt State Campus Greens and Humboldt County Green Party. For more info contact Jacob Bloom jab181@humboldt.edu or Dana Silvernale dsilver@greens.org

Tuesday, September 25 (Northern California):

Sonoma State University (1-2pm): Presentation on Social Justice with Dr. Jill Stein
Evert B. Person Theater - 1801 E Cotati Ave, Rohnert Park
Co-Sponsored by Project Censored, SSU Sociology Club, School of Social Sciences, Media Freedom Foundation, and SSU Investigative Sociology Class
 
Napa Valley College (3-4pm): Dr. Jill Stein, Green Party Presidential Candidate
2277 Napa Vallejo Highway, Napa
Dr. Jill Stein appears along with Napa Valley College student trustee Ruscal Cayangyang, SEIU representative Nathan Hansford, and a member of the conservation group Save Rural Angwin, which is backing local Napa County Measure U, an initiative that aims to regulate development in Angwin, a town in Napa County.
 
Unitarian Universalist Church of Palo Alto (7-8:30pm):  'Heathcare is a human right' - Santa Clara County Single Payer Healthcare Coalition and the Green Party of Santa Clara County Present Dr. Jill Stein, Green Party Presidential Candidate
505 Charleston Road, Palo Alto
Dr. Jill Stein, Harvard Medical School graduate and single-payer advocate will discuss the importance of health care as a human right through a Medicare for all system that provides quality comprehensive care, preserves patient choice and puts people before profits.
 

Wednesday, September 26 (Southern California):

University of San Diego (10:30-11:45am): Tired of the same old lies? It's time for a change: Meet Dr. Jill Stein
Pardee Legal Research Center, Lecture Room 132 - 5998 Alcala Park, San Diego
Sponsored by the Environmental Law Society of the University of San Diego
 
San Diego State University (12:30-2pm): Tired of the same old lies? It's time for a change: Meet Dr. Jill Stein
Tula Hall Meeting Room - 5500 Campanile Dr.,  San Diego
 
Santa Monica College (7-9pm): An Evening with U.S. Presidential candidate Dr. Jill Stein
Cayton Center - 1900 Pico Bl., Santa Monica 
 

Thursday, September 27 (Southern California):

Woodbury University (12:30-1:15pm)
7500 North Glenoaks Boulevard, Burbank 
 
Los Angeles Valley College (3:30-4:10pm) 
Monarch Hall, Campus Center - 5800 Fulton Avenue, Valley Glen 
 
Pierce College (5-5:30pm): Day of Politics/Jill Stein
Great Hall - 6201 Winnetka Avenue,  Woodland Hills
 

For more information: Jill Stein for President Campaign website
